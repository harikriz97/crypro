BankNifty Strategy:
===================
1. Take previous day high and low of BANKNIFTY FUTURES in HeinkenAshi Candle
2. Draw Gann box or Fibbonacci level from high to low
   whichever u choose should have following levels in it:
   0%    or 0 line
   25%    or 0.25 line
   38.2%  or 0.382 line
   50%    or 0.5 line
   61.8%  or 0.618 line
   75%    or 0.75 line
   100%    or 1 line
   
 My suggestion is to create excel sheet and just input high and low of previous day and calculate remaining values there itself. 
    No need of gann box drawing or fibbonacci then.

3. 50% or 0.5 line is the middle line where the BN price should trade to enter either buy or sell

4. Buy once BN crosses above 38.2% or 0.382 line. SL = 50% or 0.5 line... Targets: 25% or 0.25 line and then 0 line.

5. Sell once BN crosses below 61.8% or 0.618 line. SL = 50% or 0.5 line... Targets: 75% or 0.75 line and then 1 line.

**** Important: All this using Heiken ashi candle and in 5mins TF
